What is Happy Pets Project?

Our mission is to create a community for pet owners to share their love for animals. We want to create a community where humans and pets can come together and have fun in a safe environment. We also want to make sure that all pets are getting the attention and care that they deserve to be happy, healthy pets!

What Happy Pets Project Has Done

  1. Provide free microchipping to over 2,000 pets

  2. Food pantry events that helped feed over 500 pets this past month

  3. Brought together owners and pets and created new friendships
